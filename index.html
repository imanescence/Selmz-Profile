About

Simpsons fan and subs addict, Selmz is a DJ from France who likes to juggle between ethereal material and aggressive beats. In a nutshell, she does not beat around the bush. UK dubstep, jungle breaks, and hip-hop are her all-time favorite genres, and she loves to share her passion about melting hard basses by inviting everyone to her multi-genre journey, which feels like an infinite uppercut.
